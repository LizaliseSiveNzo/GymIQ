# Contributing to PitchIQ

PitchIQ is proprietary software solely owned by Lizalise Nzo (the "Owner").
It is **not** an open-source project and does not accept public contributions.

## No unsolicited contributions

Do not open pull requests or submit code, designs, or other materials unless
you have been expressly engaged by the Owner and have a signed agreement in
place.

## Mandatory IP assignment

Anyone who performs any work on PitchIQ — employees, contractors, freelancers,
collaborators, or advisers — **must** sign the Intellectual Property Assignment
Agreement in [`legal/IP-ASSIGNMENT-AGREEMENT.md`](legal/IP-ASSIGNMENT-AGREEMENT.md)
**before** contributing. That agreement assigns all rights in the contribution
to the Owner.

Any contribution made without a signed IP Assignment Agreement is unauthorised,
grants no rights to the contributor, and does not create any shared ownership.
By submitting anything to this project you confirm that you have such an
agreement in place and that your contribution is assigned entirely to the Owner.

## Confidentiality

The codebase and all related materials are confidential. Contributors must also
have a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (see
[`legal/NDA-TEMPLATE.md`](legal/NDA-TEMPLATE.md)) and must not disclose or reuse
any part of the project.
