**Effective date:** [date]

## 1. Purpose
The Owner may share confidential information with the Recipient so the Recipient
can [evaluate / develop / collaborate on / provide services for] PitchIQ (the
"Purpose").

## 2. Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by the
Owner, including source code, designs, database schemas, business plans,
customer and player data, credentials, and any materials marked or reasonably
understood to be confidential.

## 3. Obligations
The Recipient shall: (a) use the Confidential Information only for the Purpose;
(b) not disclose it to any third party without the Owner's prior written
consent; (c) protect it with at least reasonable care; and (d) not copy or
reproduce it except as needed for the Purpose.

## 4. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that is or becomes public
through no fault of the Recipient, was lawfully known before disclosure, or is
independently developed without use of the Confidential Information.

## 5. No Rights Granted
No licence or ownership right in the Confidential Information or in PitchIQ is
granted to the Recipient. All rights remain with the Owner.

## 6. Return or Destruction
On the Owner's request or when the Purpose ends, the Recipient shall return or
destroy all Confidential Information and copies.

## 7. Term
The confidentiality obligations continue for [e.g. 5] years from disclosure, and
indefinitely for trade secrets.

## 8. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
