## 1. Background
The Contributor has performed or will perform work relating to GymIQ,
including software development, design, documentation, or other creative or
technical work (the "Contributions").

## 2. Assignment of Rights
The Contributor irrevocably and unconditionally assigns to the Owner all
right, title, and interest, worldwide, in and to the Contributions and all
intellectual property rights in them, including copyright, database rights,
design rights, and any related rights, whether existing now or arising in the
future. This assignment includes rights in all past Contributions made before
the effective date.

## 3. Moral Rights
To the extent permitted by law, the Contributor waives any moral rights in the
Contributions in favour of the Owner and its successors.

## 4. Further Assurances
The Contributor agrees to sign any further documents and do anything reasonably
necessary to give full effect to this assignment and to help the Owner register
or enforce the assigned rights, at the Owner's expense.

## 5. Warranties
The Contributor warrants that: (a) the Contributions are original; (b) they do
not infringe any third party's rights; and (c) the Contributor has full right
and authority to make this assignment and has not previously assigned or
licensed the Contributions to anyone else.

## 6. Confidentiality
The Contributor will keep all non-public information about GymIQ confidential
and will not use it except for the benefit of the Owner.

## 7. No Retained Rights
The Contributor retains no ownership, licence, or other interest in the
Contributions or in GymIQ.

## 8. Consideration
This assignment is made in consideration of [payment / employment / engagement /
other — specify], receipt of which is acknowledged.

## 9. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
